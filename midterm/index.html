Life in the Snow

Snow is a beautiful natural phenomenon that appears during the winter season when temperatures fall below freezing. Snowflakes are formed in clouds and slowly fall to the ground, creating a soft white layer that covers landscapes.

Many people enjoy winter activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and building snowmen. Snow also plays an important role in nature because when it melts, it provides water for rivers and plants.

Interesting Snow Facts

  • Every snowflake has a unique shape.
  • Snow forms when water vapor freezes in clouds.
  • Snow helps keep plants warm during winter.
  • Heavy snowfall can change landscapes quickly.
