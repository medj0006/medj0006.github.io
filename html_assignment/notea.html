5HTML TAGS

header
Defines the header section of a document or a section, typically containing introductory content or navigation links.
nav
Represents a section of a page that links to other pages or to parts within the page; used for navigation menus.
main
Specifies the main content of a document, which is unique and central to the document's purpose.
article
Represents a self-contained composition in a document, such as a blog post, news article, or forum post.
footer
Defines the footer section of a document or a section, typically containing information about the author, copyright, or links to related documents.
